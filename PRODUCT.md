# PRODUCT.md — The Aviator Training School (TATS)

## Product Purpose
Aviation ground training institute in Trivandrum, Kerala. Prepares aspiring pilots for DGCA examinations and deploys them to EASA-certified flight schools (Gold Wings Aviation, Poland). Evidence-first: every claim backed by verifiable results.

## Register
brand

## Users
- Primary: Aspiring pilots aged 18–26 in Kerala and South India, evaluating aviation training institutes
- Secondary: Parents of cadets, co-funding the training, need trust signals
- Tertiary: Existing CPL holders seeking FI Rating (TAPP 50)

## Brand Tone
Premium, transparent, authoritative without arrogance. Never salesy. Every claim backed by evidence. "We show, not tell."

## Key Messages
- "Beyond Pilots. Shaping Aviators." — the tagline
- Evidence-backed DGCA results, every attempt, mark sheets on campus
- 32% selection rate — selective by design
- Direct EASA CPL pathway via Gold Wings Aviation, Poland
- Batch 1 flying in Poland; Batch 2 deploying June 2026

## Programmes
1. TATS 120 — 23 months, zero to EASA CPL(A), beginners
2. TATS 120 ATPL Integrated — Full ATPL theory in India, June 2026 intake
3. Fly Direct — For DGCA-cleared cadets, 15–16 months
4. TAPP 50 — For CPL holders, FI Rating pathway

## Strategic Principles
- Transparency over marketing: fees, timeline, flying school disclosed upfront
- Depth over shortcuts: 3–4 hrs daily offline in-person classes
- Evidence over reputation: no manufactured rankings
- Mentorship, not instruction: MD personally involved throughout

## Anti-References
- Generic Kerala aviation coaching institute look
- Manufactured rankings or "top results" language
- Stock aviation photos (cockpit close-ups, sunset runways)
- Glassmorphism everywhere
- SaaS hero-metric template (big number + label grid)
